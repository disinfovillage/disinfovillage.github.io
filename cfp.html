Disinformation village will hold talks, workshops and panels that will be aimed to explore disinformation from the point of view of information security.

We are looking for talks about the analysis of particular disinformation campaigns, general analysis methodology, frameworks related to computer security and disinformation and automated tools

If you can provide original interesting work related to one of the following topics:

  • The role of disinformation campaigns in cyber war and
  • The study of disinformation from the perspective of information security
  • The application of social network analysis techniques to the identification of disinformation campaigns
  • The presentation of automated tools related to identification, analysis or mitigation of disinformation

Please get in touch with us. If you wish to present at the village please send a mail with your proposal in plain text format to disinfovillage at protonmail dot com.

If you don't want to present but still want to get involved, please get in touch as well.
